Fresh Dragon Fruits

Dragon fruit is a tropical fruit that’s low in calories and high in fiber and antioxidants.

Some people say it tastes like a cross between a pear and a kiwi. Dragon fruit grows on the Hylocereus cactus, also known as the Honolulu queen, whose flowers only open at night.

Nutritional Value:

Dragon fruit contains small amounts of several nutrients. It’s also a decent source of iron, magnesium, and fiber.

Benifites of eating Dragon Fruits

Dragon fruit has a sweet and tart flavor, with a hint of citrus. It is packed with antioxidants and vitamins, making it a great addition to any meal. Enjoy it as a stand-alone snack or add it to salads, marinades, breakfasts, and desserts for a burst of flavor and color.
